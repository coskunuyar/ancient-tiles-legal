# Data Deletion Request - Ancient Tiles

**Last Updated: April 1, 2026**

Ancient Tiles ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. If you would like to request the deletion of any personal data collected (such as your Advertising ID or analytics logs), please follow the instructions below:

## How to Request Data Deletion
Please send an email to our support team with the subject line **"Data Deletion Request"**.

*   **Support Email:** [coskun.uyarr@gmail.com](mailto:coskun.uyarr@gmail.com)
*   **Method:** Provide your Advertising ID or device information if possible to help us identify your specific data records.

## What Data is Deleted?
Upon receiving your request, we will:
1.  **Wipe Analytics Activity:** Instruct Google Firebase to delete all session logs and event data associated with your device.
2.  **Clear Advertising IDs:** Instruct Google AdMob to clear your profile from our internal advertising campaigns.
3.  **Process Time:** Most deletion requests are processed within **30 days** of receipt.

## Questions?
If you have any further questions about our data practices, please reach out to us at the email address provided above.
